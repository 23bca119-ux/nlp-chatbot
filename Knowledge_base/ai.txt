What is Artificial Intelligence:
Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn.

AI Explanation:
Artificial Intelligence enables machines to mimic human thinking and learning processes.

What AI Enables:
AI enables machines to perform tasks such as problem solving.

AI Task Problem Solving:
AI systems can analyze situations and find solutions to problems.

AI Task Decision Making:
AI can make decisions based on data and logic.

AI Task Speech Recognition:
AI can recognize and process human speech.

AI Task Language Understanding:
AI can understand human language using NLP.

AI Task Image Recognition:
AI can identify objects and patterns in images.

Types of Artificial Intelligence:
There are different types of AI such as Narrow AI and General AI.

What is Narrow AI:
Narrow AI is designed to perform specific tasks.

Narrow AI Explanation:
Narrow AI focuses on a single task like voice assistants or recommendation systems.

What is General AI:
General AI is capable of performing any intellectual task like humans.

General AI Explanation:
General AI can think, learn, and adapt across multiple domains like human intelligence.

What is Machine Learning:
Machine Learning is a subset of AI that allows systems to learn patterns from data.

Machine Learning Explanation:
Machine Learning enables systems to improve automatically through experience.

What is Deep Learning:
Deep Learning is a specialized form of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers.

Deep Learning Explanation:
Deep Learning uses complex neural networks to process large amounts of data.

Examples of Artificial Intelligence:
Examples of AI include self-driving cars.

AI Example Self Driving Cars:
Self-driving cars use AI to navigate and make driving decisions.

AI Example Chatbots:
Chatbots use AI to simulate conversations with users.

AI Example Recommendation Systems:
Recommendation systems suggest products or content based on user behavior.
