Chatbots
A chatbot is a computer program designed to simulate conversation with human users through text or voice.
Chatbots use Natural Language Processing to understand user questions and provide responses.
Types of Chatbots:
• Rule-based chatbots
• AI-powered chatbots
• Hybrid chatbots
Advantages of Chatbots:
• Available 24/7
• Instant responses
• Cost efficient
• Can handle multiple users
Chatbots are commonly used in:
• Customer service
• Education
• Banking
• Healthcare
• E-commerce
