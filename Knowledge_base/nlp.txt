What is NLP:
Natural Language Processing is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that helps computers understand, interpret, and generate human language.

NLP Explanation:
NLP combines linguistics, computer science, and machine learning to process human language.

Applications of NLP:
NLP is used in chatbots.

NLP Application Chatbots:
Chatbots are a major application of NLP used for automated conversations.

NLP Application Machine Translation:
Machine translation converts text from one language to another.

NLP Application Voice Assistants:
Voice assistants like Alexa and Siri use NLP to understand voice commands.

NLP Application Sentiment Analysis:
Sentiment analysis detects emotions in text.

NLP Application Text Summarization:
Text summarization reduces long text into shorter meaningful summaries.

NLP Application Spam Detection:
Spam detection identifies unwanted or harmful messages.

What is Tokenization:
Tokenization is the process of splitting text into words or sentences.

Tokenization Explanation:
Tokenization breaks text into individual words or tokens.

What is Stemming:
Stemming reduces words to their root form.

Stemming Explanation:
Stemming removes suffixes to get the base form of a word.

What is Lemmatization:
Lemmatization converts words to their base or dictionary form.

Lemmatization Explanation:
Lemmatization uses vocabulary and grammar rules to find the correct base form.

What is Named Entity Recognition:
Named Entity Recognition identifies names, locations, and organizations in text.

NER Explanation:
NER helps in extracting important entities from text.

What is Part-of-Speech Tagging:
Part-of-Speech tagging identifies grammatical roles of words.

POS Tagging Explanation:
POS tagging assigns labels like noun, verb, adjective to words.
